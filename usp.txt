Unique Selling Proposition Of lnc-SCOPE
(From structure to function)

CORE USP: Dual-Layer Structural + Sequential Interaction Analysis
- ViennaRNA: predicts RNA structure but does not directly evaluate miRNA biological targeting.
- miRNA tools (like seed-only scanners): sequence complementarity but often ignore lncRNA structural accessibility
- This tool combines both and takes everything into consideration

Unlike conventional tools that focus either on RNA secondary structure or sequence complementarity alone, LNC-Scope integrates structural accessibility with seed-based and thermodynamic miRNA-lncRNA interaction analysis.

Unique features of lnc-Scope include:
- Structural + Sequential dual analysis
- Seed-compatible accessibility filtering
- Functional interpretation (sponge/scaffold)
- Mutation sensitivity
- End-to-end lncRNA-specific pipeline

Our platform extends beyond conventional RNA folding by integrating structure-informed accessibility, sequence-level miRNA targeting, thermodynamic scoring, and functional lncRNA interpretation within a single pipeline.
